About Brawl Stars

A fast-paced multiplayer battle game by Supercell

Multiplayer Battles & Unique Characters

Join millions of players worldwide in quick and action-packed battles. Choose from a variety of Brawlers, each with their unique abilities and playstyles.

  • Developer: Supercell
  • Release Date: December 12, 2018
  • Platform: iOS, Android
  • Genre: Multiplayer, Action
  • Game Modes: Solo, Duo, Team
